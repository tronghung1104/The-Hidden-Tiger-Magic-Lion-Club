The Hidden Tiger Magic Lion Club

The Club

The Hidden Tiger Magic Lion Club is our local free group where young people can learn how to code, develop websites, apps, programs, games and more.

The HTML Club is led by volunteers and it depends on the community to run because it is absolutely free.

To The Parents

All Parents must attend their first child's first class to sign the parental permission. Parents of under 12 must stay on the premises at all times.

Please be advised that places will be limited to ticket holders only: One Ticket Per Child.

Location and Hours

Dublin, Ireland.

Friday and Saturday 2pm to 4pm.
